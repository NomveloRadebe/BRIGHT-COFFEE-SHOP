# BRIGHT-COFFEE-SHOP

Case study to provide insights to the CEO of Bright Coffee Shop about sales. 

Case Study Synopsis: Sales Analysis of Bright Coffee Shop
In order to assist a recently appointed CEO who wants to boost sales and improve product performance, the Bright Coffee Shop Sales case study focuses on evaluating daily transactional data. Converting past sales data into useful insights that guide strategic decision-making is the responsibility of a junior data analyst.
Finding top-performing products, identifying peak sales times throughout the day, and identifying patterns across several product categories and time periods are the objectives of the analysis. The objective is to highlight important performance drivers within the company by utilizing data analytics, SQL, and visualization tools.
In the conclusion, the project will provide data-driven suggestions to maximize sales, boost operational effectiveness, and assist the coffee shop's revenue growth.
